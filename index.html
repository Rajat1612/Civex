About Civex Technical Services

Civex Technical Services Pvt. Ltd. is a specialized civil engineering consultancy firm dedicated to providing comprehensive technical support for tendering, contract management, and project closure services. We serve government agencies, EPC contractors, and infrastructure clients with precision, compliance, and professional excellence.

Our team of experienced engineers and technical experts ensures that every project receives meticulous attention to detail, from initial bid preparation through project completion and contract closure. We combine technical expertise with regulatory knowledge to deliver solutions that meet the highest standards of accuracy and compliance.

What We Do

Tendering Support

End-to-end assistance for contractors who lack registrations, experience, or documentation required to bid successfully.

Contract Management

Practical contract administration to control scope, timelines, payments, and employer obligations during execution.

Variation & EOT Management

Identification, documentation, and submission of variations and time extensions to protect entitlement and cash flow.

Claims & Dispute Support

Structured claim preparation and negotiation support after completion, without litigation-heavy processes.

Contract Closure

Final bill certification, statement of claims, and smooth closure to avoid long-term payment disputes.
