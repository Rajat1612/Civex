Our Services

Comprehensive technical support for civil and infrastructure projects

A. Bidder Registration

We provide comprehensive support for bidder registration on government portals and private procurement systems, ensuring compliance and eligibility throughout the registration process.

Key Services:

  • Registration Support: Complete assistance with registration on government portals including GeM, CPP Portal, e-Procurement systems, and other designated platforms.
  • Eligibility Documentation: Preparation and verification of all required eligibility documents including company registration, financial statements, technical credentials, and compliance certificates.
  • Vendor Onboarding: Systematic vendor onboarding process including document submission, verification, and activation of vendor accounts.
  • Compliance Checks: Thorough compliance verification to ensure all regulatory requirements are met before registration and bidding.
  • Support Services: Guidance and assistance for both new and experienced bidders, including portal navigation, document upload, and account management.

B. Tendering Solution

Our tendering solutions cover the entire bidding process from identification to submission, ensuring comprehensive preparation and optimal bid competitiveness.

Service Components:

  • Tender Identification: Systematic identification and analysis of relevant tenders matching client capabilities and strategic objectives.
  • Eligibility Evaluation: Detailed assessment of tender eligibility criteria including technical qualifications, financial capacity, and past performance requirements.
  • Technical Bid Preparation: Comprehensive technical bid preparation including project methodology, work plans, technical specifications, and quality assurance frameworks.
  • Financial Bid Preparation: Accurate financial bid preparation with detailed cost estimation, pricing strategies, and financial compliance documentation.
  • BOQ Review and Estimation: Thorough review of Bill of Quantities (BOQ), quantity take-off, rate analysis, and cost estimation in accordance with standard schedules and market rates.
  • Pre-bid Clarification Support: Assistance in preparing and submitting pre-bid queries, attending pre-bid meetings, and addressing clarifications.
  • Online Tender Submission: Complete support for online tender submission including document preparation, digital signatures, and submission verification.

C. Contract Management

Comprehensive contract management services covering site operations, quality assurance, and material management to ensure project execution excellence.

a) Site Management

  • Progress Monitoring: Regular monitoring and documentation of project progress against planned schedules and milestones.
  • Stakeholder Coordination: Effective coordination between contractors, consultants, clients, and other project stakeholders.
  • Documentation and Reporting: Systematic documentation of site activities, progress reports, meeting minutes, and status updates.
  • Schedule Tracking: Continuous tracking of project schedules, critical path analysis, and identification of potential delays with mitigation strategies.

b) Quality Management

  • QA/QC Documentation: Preparation and maintenance of Quality Assurance and Quality Control documentation including test reports, inspection records, and quality plans.
  • Compliance with Specifications: Verification and documentation of compliance with project specifications, codes, and standards.
  • Inspection Records: Systematic recording of material inspections, work inspections, and quality audits throughout project execution.
  • Quality Audits and Reports: Conducting quality audits, preparing audit reports, and implementing corrective actions to maintain quality standards.

c) Material Management

  • Material Reconciliation: Accurate reconciliation of material quantities received, consumed, and remaining at project sites.
  • Consumption Tracking: Detailed tracking of material consumption against BOQ and work progress to ensure optimal utilization.
  • Inventory Records: Maintenance of comprehensive inventory records including material receipts, issues, transfers, and stock levels.
  • Compliance with Approved Materials: Verification that all materials used comply with approved material lists and specifications.

D. Variation Management

Professional management of contract variations including identification, documentation, rate analysis, and approval coordination to protect client interests.

Service Elements:

  • Identification of Variations: Systematic identification of variations from original contract scope, including extra items, omissions, and modifications.
  • Documentation of Extra Items: Comprehensive documentation of extra items including work descriptions, quantities, and supporting evidence.
  • Rate Analysis: Detailed rate analysis for variation items based on contract rates, market rates, and justified pricing methodologies.
  • Approval Coordination: Coordination with clients and consultants for obtaining approvals for variations, including submission of proposals and negotiation support.
  • Financial Impact Assessment: Assessment of financial impact of variations on project cost, timeline, and overall contract value.

E. Claim Management (After Completion of Work)

Professional claim management services for post-completion disputes through conciliation, mediation, and arbitration processes with technical documentation and expert support.

a) Conciliation

  • Assisted Negotiation: Support in negotiating claims and disputes with clients through structured conciliation processes.
  • Issue Resolution Support: Technical and documentation support to resolve issues and reach mutually acceptable settlements.
  • Documentation and Representation: Preparation of comprehensive claim documentation and technical representation during conciliation proceedings.

b) Mediation

  • Neutral Technical Assessment: Provision of neutral technical assessment and expert opinions to facilitate mediation processes.
  • Structured Dispute Resolution: Support in structured mediation processes with technical evidence and documentation.
  • Cost-Effective Settlement Approach: Facilitation of cost-effective settlement approaches through technical clarity and evidence-based arguments.

c) Arbitration

  • Technical Documentation: Preparation of comprehensive technical documentation for arbitration proceedings including claim statements, counter-statements, and evidence.
  • Claim Substantiation: Technical substantiation of claims with engineering calculations, drawings, specifications, and supporting documents.
  • Support to Legal Teams: Technical support to legal teams including preparation of technical arguments, expert witness preparation, and documentation review.
  • Expert Engineering Inputs: Provision of expert engineering inputs, analysis, and opinions to strengthen claim positions in arbitration.

F. Contract Closure

Comprehensive contract closure services ensuring proper documentation, reconciliation, and compliance for successful project completion and handover.

Closure Activities:

  • Final Bill Preparation: Preparation of final bills including all executed work, variations, and adjustments in accordance with contract terms.
  • Reconciliation of Quantities: Comprehensive reconciliation of quantities executed against BOQ, variations, and measurement records.
  • Completion Documentation: Preparation of project completion documentation including completion certificates, as-built drawings, and operation manuals.
  • Handing-Over Records: Systematic preparation of handing-over records, asset registers, and transfer documentation for project handover.
  • Contract Close-Out Compliance: Ensuring compliance with all contract close-out requirements including final payments, retention release, and warranty documentation.
