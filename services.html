Our Services

Comprehensive technical and commercial support for contractors across the entire contract lifecycle.

Tendering Support

Complete bid assistance including BOQ review, eligibility checks, bid strategy development, and tender documentation support.

Contract Management

Practical contract administration support to protect contractor interests during project execution and day-to-day operations.

How We Support Contractors

Pre-Contract Stage

We assist contractors during tendering by reviewing BOQs, identifying risks, checking eligibility criteria, and preparing technically compliant submissions.

Execution Stage

During execution, we support contract administration, measurements, variations, correspondence, and commercial tracking to safeguard contractor interests.

Payment & Time Management

We help contractors manage running bills, payment follow-ups, EOT documentation, and delay justifications under contract provisions.

Claims & Closure

At project completion, we assist in claims preparation, final bill settlement, reconciliation, and smooth contract closure.
